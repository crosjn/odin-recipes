Family Favorite Oatmeal

Description

Fast microwaved oatmeal uses 10-minute oats cooked perfectly. By using bananas and spices, we omitted any processed sugars or sweetners. Our kids still LOVED this (and still do as teenagers!)

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Oatmeal (10 minutes oats)
  • 2 Cups Water
  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 2 Bananas - browner is better!
  • 2 Tbsps Cinnamon (or more to taste)

Directions

  1. In a glass 4-cup measuring cup, add the water, milk, and oatmeal, stirring to combine.
  2. Microwave on full power for 4-1/2 minutes. You want it to just start boiling and need to watch it to be sure that it does, and does NOT boil over
  3. Stir contents and microwave on 50% power for 2 minutes. Again, you want it to boil but not boil over.
  4. Stir contents again, return to microwave and microwave on 20% power for 6 minutes. This is the equivalent of a stove-top simmer.
  5. Crush the banana to a pulp. Stir in the bananas and oatmeal
  6. Optional, dress with fresh berries to make a picture-perfect bowl!
