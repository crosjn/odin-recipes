Classic Grilled Hamburger

Description

Just like the title says! Who doesn't love the American classic burger?!

Ingredients

  • Burgers
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Sliced Onions
  • Cheese
  • Condiments: Mustard, Ketchup... You decide!

Directions

  1. Fire up the grill and let it reach medium-high heat
  2. Grill burgers
  3. Place burger on bun
  4. Dress your burger with the fancy fixings you've laid out!
