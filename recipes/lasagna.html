Mom's Thirty Minute Lasanga

Description

Develop all the flavors you remember as a kid with this delicious lasagna that is ready in a flash. So much easier than traditional lasagna but just a yummy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 fresh garlic cloves
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups jarred marinara sauce
  • 1-1/2 cups water
  • 8 dry lasagna noodles broken up
  • 1 cup cottage cheese

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beed. Stir in the onions and garlic and continue cooking until the beef is cooked and the onions are translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Drain any excess grease.
  3. Stir in the marinara and water, then bring mixture to a boil.
  4. Turn heat to low, nestle in the noodles (broken for placement), cover and simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Stir in the cottage cheese, recover, and simmer another 10-12 minutes or until noodles are tender.
